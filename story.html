My Journey

I had a tough time in my Educational Journey. But the way I handled it made me the person I am Today.

Summary of My Personal Story:

My journey as a Mathematician and an Industrial Design, Electronics and Computer engineer began with a childhood fascination for both technology and aesthetics. I wanted to create physical products that seamlessly integrated technology and design, products that not only functioned flawlessly but also appealed to users on an aesthetic and emotional level.

I pursued a dual degree in Electronics and Computer Engineering, immersing myself in courses that ranged from human-centered design to electronics and hardware prototyping. I'm excited to embark on new projects, collaborate with like-minded individuals, and continue pushing the boundaries of what's possible in the field of electronics and Computer engineering.
