Pesonal Story

Summary of My Personal Story:

My journey as a computer and industrial design engineer began with a childhood fascination for both technology and aesthetics.
I wanted to create physical products that seamlessly integrated technology and design, products that not only functioned flawlessly but also appealed to users on an aesthetic and emotional level.
I pursued a dual degree in computer science and industrial design, immersing myself in courses that ranged from human-centered design to electronics and hardware prototyping.
I'm excited to embark on new projects, collaborate with like-minded individuals, and continue pushing the boundaries of what's possible in the field of computer and industrial design engineering.
